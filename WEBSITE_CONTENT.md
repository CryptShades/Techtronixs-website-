### FAQ — Common Questions

*Everything you need to know before reaching out.*

---

**How quickly can you start on a new project?**
> Most projects start within 1–2 weeks of contract signing. For urgent engagements we can often begin within 72 hours — just tell us in your message.

**What is your typical project timeline?**
> MVPs and focused feature builds usually take 6–12 weeks. Full-stack enterprise systems run 3–6 months. Every project gets a written delivery timeline in the proposal.

**Do you work with early-stage startups?**
> Yes — roughly 30% of our clients are pre-Series A. We offer a startup-friendly engagement model with milestone-based billing so you're not fronting large sums before you've seen the work.

**What does the discovery process look like?**
> Discovery is a 1–2 week paid phase where we map your requirements, review any existing systems, and produce a detailed technical specification and project plan. You keep the artefacts regardless of whether you proceed.

**How do you handle project pricing?**
> We offer three models: fixed-price (for well-scoped work), time & materials (for evolving requirements), and dedicated squad retainers for ongoing product development. We'll recommend the right model after discovery.

**What tech stack do you work with?**
> We are stack-agnostic and choose the right tool for the problem. Our most common front-end stack is React/Next.js; backend we use Node.js, Python, and Go; infrastructure is primarily AWS and GCP with Terraform.

**Do you take over existing codebases?**
> Yes. We conduct a paid codebase audit first to identify risks and estimate remediation effort. We then provide a written report before any development begins so there are no surprises.

**What happens after the project launches?**
> We include 30 days of post-launch support in every engagement. After that, clients can move to a structured maintenance retainer covering monitoring, security patches, and priority access to the team.

**Is our IP protected when working with NovaTech?**
> Absolutely. All IP created during the engagement is assigned to you on full payment. Our standard contract includes a full IP assignment clause and a mutual NDA. Your idea stays yours.
